About Me vs. Me

Me vs. Me was created to help individuals improve their lives by competing against their past selves. Track your habits, mood and goals to become the best version of yourself.

Our philosophy is simple: continuous improvement is the key to personal growth. By providing a minimalist platform, we aim to remove distractions and focus solely on what matters – your daily wins and long-term progress.

Join us on this journey to self-mastery. Every day is a new opportunity to be better than you were yesterday.
